# 🚀 Customer Churn Intelligence System  
### 📊 Data Analytics Internship Project

---

## 🧠 Project Summary

During my **Data Analytics Internship**, I worked on a real-world telecom dataset to analyze, understand, and predict customer churn.

The objective was not just model building, but converting raw data into **actionable business insights** to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Customer churn leads to significant revenue loss.

This project focuses on:
- Identifying key factors behind churn  
- Predicting at-risk customers  
- Recommending strategies to reduce churn  

---

## ⚙️ Workflow

### 🔹 Data Preparation
- Handled missing values and inconsistencies  
- Encoded categorical variables  
- Prepared dataset for analysis  

### 🔹 Exploratory Analysis
- Analyzed customer behavior across features  
- Compared churn vs non-churn patterns  

### 🔹 Customer Segmentation
- Grouped customers based on tenure, charges, and services  
- Identified high-risk segments  

### 🔹 Predictive Modeling
- Applied Logistic Regression & Decision Trees  
- Evaluated using performance metrics  

### 🔹 Business Insights
- Translated findings into real-world retention strategies  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Customers with **low tenure and high monthly charges** are most likely to churn  
- **Month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate  
- Customers without premium services show higher churn behavior  

---

## 📈 Model Performance

| Metric     | Score  |
|------------|--------|
| Accuracy   | 86.5%  |
| Precision  | 84.2%  |
| Recall     | 88.7%  |
| F1 Score   | 86.4%  |

> Model is optimized for **high recall** to capture maximum churn cases.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Target high-risk customers with personalized offers  
- Encourage long-term subscriptions  
- Provide bundled services for better engagement  
- Focus on early-stage customers (0–6 months tenure)
